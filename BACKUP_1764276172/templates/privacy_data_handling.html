Data Handling & Privacy Policy

How We Protect Your Data

Security Measures

  • Encryption: All uploaded files encrypted at rest
  • Access Control: Only authorized Rouze team can access data
  • Secure Storage: Files stored on isolated secure server
  • Audit Logging: All access logged (who, when, what action)
  • No Sharing: Your data never shared with third parties

Automatic Deletion

All uploaded files automatically deleted after 30 days. You can request deletion anytime before then.

Audit Trail

We maintain complete audit log of all file operations (upload, access, deletion). Available for compliance verification.

Your Responsibilities

You are responsible for:

  • Only uploading data you have legal rights to share
  • Removing personally identifiable information (names, emails, phone numbers)
  • Removing payment information or financial account data
  • Removing healthcare data (unless anonymized)
  • Removing government IDs or SSNs
  • Complying with your own data retention policies
  • Ensuring you're not violating any agreements with your customers

Rouze is NOT liable for:

  • Data you upload without authorization
  • Regulatory violations you cause by uploading restricted data
  • Breach of confidentiality agreements you're under
  • Misuse of data you're not supposed to have

Rouze IS responsible for:

  • Secure encryption and storage
  • Preventing unauthorized access
  • Automatic deletion on schedule
  • Audit trail maintenance
  • GDPR compliance for data we control

GDPR & Compliance

If you have customers in the EU:

  • Rouze acts as a Data Processor
  • You act as Data Controller (responsible for compliance)
  • You must have consent from your customers before sharing their data
  • You have the right to request deletion anytime (we will comply)
  • We maintain Data Processing Agreement (DPA) upon request

Your Rights:

  • Right to Access: You can request to see what data we have on you
  • Right to Delete: You can request complete deletion anytime
  • Right to Portability: You can request your data in standard format
  • Right to Audit: You can request audit log of access

Privacy Requests

For any data privacy requests (access, deletion, DPA, compliance):

Email: privacy@rouze-intelligence.com

We respond to all privacy requests within 7 business days.

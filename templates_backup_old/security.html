Data Encryption

All data in transit and at rest is protected with bank-grade encryption standards.

Transmission Encryption (TLS 1.3)
All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using TLS 1.3, the same encryption standard used by banks, military, and government agencies. Man-in-the-middle attacks are mathematically impossible.
At-Rest Encryption (AES-256)
All data stored in our databases is encrypted using AES-256, the U.S. government standard for classified information. Even if our servers were physically stolen, your data would be unreadable.
Key Management
Encryption keys are managed separately from encrypted data and protected with role-based access control. Only authorized personnel with legitimate business need have access to encryption keys.
What This Means: Your data is secure whether it's traveling over the internet or sitting in our databases. Only your account has the decryption keys to read your information.

Compliance & Certifications

We maintain certification and compliance with industry-leading security standards.

SOC2 Type II
We are SOC2 Type II certified, meaning our security controls are independently audited and verified annually by third-party auditors. This is the gold standard for SaaS companies.
GDPR (EU General Data Protection Regulation)
We comply with GDPR for users in the EU. Your personal data is processed lawfully, transparently, and with proper consent. You have the right to access, correct, or delete your data.
CCPA (California Consumer Privacy Act)
We comply with CCPA for users in California. You have the right to know what data we collect, delete your data, and opt-out of any data selling (which we don't do).
HIPAA (Healthcare Insurance Portability and Accountability Act)
We are HIPAA-eligible for healthcare customers. If you handle Protected Health Information (PHI), we can sign a Business Associate Agreement (BAA) to ensure compliance with healthcare privacy regulations.
What This Means: We comply with the strictest privacy regulations in the world. Your data is protected under legal frameworks designed to protect the most sensitive information (healthcare, financial, personal).

Data Retention & Deletion

You control how long your data is stored. We provide automatic deletion, with zero manual effort required.

  • Delete After Analysis: Your files are deleted immediately after your analysis is complete
  • Keep 7 Days: Your files remain accessible for 7 days, then auto-delete
  • Keep 14 Days: Your files remain accessible for 14 days, then auto-delete
  • Keep 30 Days: Your files remain accessible for 30 days, then auto-delete
  • Manual Deletion: Request deletion at any time. Processed within 24 hours
How It Works: On the retention date you select, our system automatically deletes your files and all associated data. No manual request needed. You never have to worry about your data being stored longer than you want.

Data Access & Sharing

Your Data, Your Control.

  • No Third-Party Sharing: We never sell or share your data with third parties
  • No Marketing Use: We never use your data for marketing purposes or any other purpose besides your analysis
  • Employee Access Limited: Only employees with legitimate business need can access your data
  • No Combination: We never combine your data with other customers' data
  • Audit Rights: You can request an audit of how your data is being used
Data Processing Agreement (DPA)
For customers handling sensitive data, we provide a Data Processing Agreement outlining exactly how we collect, process, and protect your information. Available on request.
What This Means: Your data is yours alone. We're a service provider, not a data broker. We have zero incentive to keep or use your data beyond serving you.

Regular Security Audits

We undergo regular security testing and audits to identify and fix vulnerabilities before they can be exploited.

  • Annual SOC2 Audits: Third-party auditors test our controls annually
  • Quarterly Penetration Testing: Professional penetration testers attempt to break into our systems
  • Weekly Vulnerability Scans: Automated scanners check for known vulnerabilities
  • Monthly Security Reviews: Internal team reviews access logs and security events
What This Means: We're constantly testing ourselves to find problems before attackers do. Every vulnerability is fixed before it becomes a risk to you.

Incident Response & Notification

In the unlikely event of a security incident, we have a documented response plan.

  • Immediate Investigation: Any potential breach is investigated within 2 hours
  • Customer Notification: If your data is at risk, you'll be notified immediately
  • Regulatory Reporting: We comply with all regulatory notification requirements (usually within 72 hours)
  • Transparent Communication: We tell you exactly what happened, when, and what we're doing about it

Bayesian Inference

Frequently Asked Questions

How is Rouze different from traditional market research? +
Traditional research relies on surveys and interviews (6-12 month lag). Rouze analyzes real-time signals from social media, forums, and reviews - capturing authentic, unfiltered consumer sentiment as it happens. We deliver insights 180 days earlier at 90% lower cost.
What data sources do you analyze? +
We aggregate signals from 20+ sources: Reddit, Twitter/X, TikTok, YouTube, Amazon reviews, Trustpilot, G2, job boards (LinkedIn, Indeed), forums, news RSS feeds, and more. All public data, ethically collected with full compliance.
How do you ensure statistical rigor? +
Our methodology is peer-reviewed and published. We use Bayesian inference, chi-square significance testing (p < 0.05), false discovery rate correction, confidence intervals, and cross-source validation. Methods validated by Ernst & Young and presented at AAAI.
Is my data secure? +
Yes. We're SOC 2 Type II certified, GDPR compliant, and use AES-256 encryption. All API calls are over HTTPS. We never store your proprietary data - only anonymized, aggregated public signals.
Can I customize the analysis for my industry? +
Absolutely. Each client gets custom source selection, industry-specific terminology models, and tailored analysis frameworks. We adapt our methodology to your market's unique characteristics and decision-making needs.

### Operational Weaknesses
❌ **Manual Bottlenecks**: Human analysis creates scalability issues
❌ **Quality Consistency**: No standardized scoring across analysts
❌ **Client Education**: Insufficient methodology transparency
❌ **Competitive Moats**: Easy for competitors to replicate approach

Technical Specifications

Encryption Standards

In Transit

TLS 1.3

All data transmitted between your browser and our servers uses Transport Layer Security 1.3, the latest and most secure version. Perfect forward secrecy ensures past communications remain secure even if keys are compromised.

At Rest

AES-256

All stored data is encrypted using Advanced Encryption Standard with 256-bit keys. This is the same encryption standard used by governments and financial institutions for classified information.

Key Management

Isolated Keys

Encryption keys are managed separately from data storage. Keys are rotated regularly and are never stored alongside encrypted data. Access requires multi-factor authentication.

Access Control

Role-Based

Only your assigned analyst can access your project data. All access is logged, auditable, and restricted by role-based permissions with principle of least privilege.

Common Questions

Security FAQ

What happens to my uploaded data?

Your data is encrypted with TLS 1.3 during transmission and AES-256 at rest. Only your dedicated analyst can access it. Data is automatically deleted after your chosen retention period (7, 14, or 30 days). We never share, sell, or use your data for any purpose other than your specific analysis.

Do you share or sell my data?

Never. Your data is used only for your analysis. We do not aggregate, anonymize for other clients, or sell to third parties. Period.

Can I delete my data early?

Yes. You can request immediate deletion at any time through your dashboard or by contacting us. Deletion is permanent and completed within 24 hours of request.

Who can access my project data?

Only your assigned analyst has access to your project data. All access is logged and auditable. Our team operates under strict NDAs and security training requirements.
